# HRSignal v1 Spec (Frozen)

## Product
India-first HR software marketplace for SMEs/MSMEs with stack-building recommendations and lead monetization.

## Positioning (frozen)
**Headline:** Build your HR stack for India—HRMS, payroll, hiring & more—picked to fit your team.

**Proof points:**
- India-first realities: compliance readiness, multi-state operations, implementation + support quality.
- Fit-based shortlists: team size, workforce mix, budget, must-have integrations.
- Buy faster: comparisons + demo questions + expert assist.

## v1 categories (frozen)
- HRMS / Core HR
- Payroll & Compliance
- Recruitment / ATS
- Background Verification (BGV)
- Performance & OKRs
- Engagement & Surveys
- LMS / Training
- Time & Attendance / Shift Scheduling

## Buyer cohorts
- 20–200
- 50–500
- 100–1000

## Monetization + routing (frozen)
- **Single best-fit vendor** per lead.
- Lead created -> auto-assign vendor -> **queued for internal review**.
- Admin manually clicks **Send to vendor**.
- Track deliveries and lead status internally.

## v1 Admin
Internal-only admin login (credentials via env). No vendor self-serve login.
